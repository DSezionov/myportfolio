Tools

Figma, Adobe Illustrator, Adobe Photoshop, Claude, ChatGPT

The Challenge

ProDefence approached me with a clear challenge: beyond a logo, the brand had no coherent visual identity system in place. Their social media presence felt disjointed and unprofessional, with posts lacking any visual consistency or polish. There were no corporate elements, no merchandise, no presentation templates — essentially no visual language to represent the brand across any touchpoint.

The Approach

The process began with research into the target audience and competitive landscape, paired with a deep dive into ProDefence's core service areas — comprehensive security, cybersecurity, crypto-asset recovery, and legal protection. Based on this research, I developed several branding directions before refining the concept into a system that would communicate authority, protection, and trust across every format.

The Identity

The resulting identity is anchored by a refined logotype and mark that communicate precision and reliability. The typographic system pairs Gilroy for headlines — bold and structural — with Inter as the primary body typeface, balancing authority with readability. The colour palette centres on a striking red against a black background, with red as the dominant accent: a deliberate choice to capture the emotional intensity of security and danger, while reinforcing ProDefence's role in resolving exactly these kinds of high-stakes situations.

The System

Beyond the core identity, I built a comprehensive brand system including a full icon set, and templates for documents, presentations, and social media — all backed by a detailed brand book covering every aspect of how the identity should be applied. The system also extends into physical production: a complete merchandise line for employees, press credentials for affiliated journalists, presentation booklets for conferences, exhibition stands, and other commercial print materials. I also designed logos and visual identities for ProDefence's sub-projects, including its legal support division, journalist association, and affiliated NGOs — extending the brand system across a wide network of related initiatives.

The Outcome

ProDefence launched its new identity in 2024, and I've continued to develop and refine the system ever since. The brand is now consistently present both online and offline — visible across social media and in physical materials at events and partner locations. Since the rebrand, the company has seen a significant increase in brand recognition, growth in its social media audience, and an expanding client base.
